Reflected Optical Camera Communication Using Uniform LED Light

IoT Laboratory, Sichuan University2019/09-now

Yanbing Yang, associate Professor

  • Optical Camera Communication systems have to come over challenges in the low achievable throughput, too professional equipment and instability in a variable environment when come from laboratory to practical use.
  • By adopting a hybrid modulation scheme for generating multi-level pulse amplitude modulation (M-PAM) symbols, achieve much higher data rate than existing works with a single small-size LED spotlight.
  • Build a high performance transmitter with a commercial-off-the-shelf LED luminaire with a lens driven by FPGA and develop an application, which supports common mobile phone camera to be used as a receiver, which greatly reduced the requirements for equipments.
  • Demodulation is proceeded by a machine learning based classifier which can automatically adapt the channel change within reasonable communication distance and viewing angle.
